### 4. App Description

**Short Description (80 chars):**
Smart mutual fund recommendations based on your goals and risk profile

**Full Description:**
MF Advisor provides personalized mutual fund recommendations using AI-powered analysis of 4 key factors:

✓ Risk Tolerance - Match your comfort level
✓ Investment Horizon - Short, medium, or long-term
✓ Investment Goals - Retirement, wealth, tax saving
✓ Budget Optimization - Dynamic fund allocation

Features:
• Real-time NAV data from verified sources
• 1-5+ fund recommendations based on investment amount
• Historical performance metrics (1Y, 3Y, 5Y)
• Direct investment links
• Beautiful, intuitive interface

Perfect for both new and experienced investors looking to make informed decisions.
